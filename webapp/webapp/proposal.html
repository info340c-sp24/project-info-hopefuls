Workout Planner Proposal

Why workout planners aren't working

Problem/Data Domain

Nowadays a lot of apps that are marketed as fitness pal apps have paywalls that make it impossible for users to use the app for its intended use. Newcomers and veteran gym-goers most likely already pay for gym memberships and would like to avoid buying other memberships, especially with something as simple as a gym tracker. A lot of these apps also have too many features, making it confusing to use. Apps like “Strong Workout Tracker Gym Log” are loaded with features that are helpful for newcomers but people with more experience would like to simply have a place to plan and log in their workouts in a simple manner. Though many apps do provide ways to plan and log, they often don’t give users exactly what they need. They include calorie tracking, premade workout videos, generic questions about your goals, meal planning, body measurement trackers, etc. This is overwhelming information and for a lot of veteran gym goers, it turns obsolete. So what are the features that would get the most interactions? What if there was a web app that simply had those most used features? Most fitness apps have preset workouts for their users, this is ineffective because workouts should be customized to each person and their needs. Creating your own workouts should be a key feature and being able to have a fully customizable set of workouts for each user is the most effective way to make sure users have their OWN workouts at their disposal to revisit, share, and post. Nothing sucks more than buying an app membership and being stuck with generic workouts that you can't change. Being able to create your own workouts, plan a week out based on your customized workouts, create your own catalog of workouts that work for YOU, and share those with friends and other users is the sole purpose of a workout planner. There is no need for gimmicks that you barely use or workouts that aren't giving you the results you need.

App Description:

Who are the users of the application? This application is used by gym goers wether it be newer beginners or if they are true gym veterans. This app ranges heavily with the gym audience it represents. This will allow all gym goes no matter what group or social class to have the same access to this app that allows for more efficient workout planning. Due to the app being free as well as the app allowing modification to your workouts it can help everybody grow and get better physically and mentally accounting for accessibility to all users and gym goers. It also accounts for everybody's purpose of workout in the gym by allowing workouts to be chosen by you.

Key Features:

What will the users do with the web app? They will be able to work on their fitness journey by using these key features:

  • Serach for workouts: Users can search for workouts to add into their own personalized workout plan.
  • Customized Workout Plans: Users can create personalized workout plans for their own fitness goals and preferences.
  • Organize pre-set workouts: Users can organize their workouts into a week plan to plan ahead for their week.

What kind of information will the users be viewing? The users will be viewing and interacting with the following information: workout plans, excercise catalog, workout logs, and other content. This will allow the user to be able to get all the information that they need in order to use the workout app. The different information that the users will be getting will allow them to gain workout knowledge as well as allow themselves to keep up with going to the gym.

How will the app help solve the problem? The app will address the issues that were introduced above. It will help with simplying the user experience. This starts with getting rid of uneccesary features that dont help the user and will make a user friendly interface that appeals to the users. It will also be customizable allowing the user to make there own adjustments to the app to fit their own personal goals and aspirations. The app will also avoid paywalls making it so any user from anywhere can use the app for free. It can also connect you with other users and you can share/post which can foster a community of gym goers.
